Account & Data Deletion Request

At Hilmost Enterprises, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your Ultimate TicTacToe account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to request deletion

To ensure the security of your account, we require requests to come from the email address associated with your game account.

  • Email us at: keeplife.munyede@hilmost.net
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request - [Your Player Name/Email]
  • Timeline: Once verified, we will delete your Firebase UID, Authentication record, and all associated gameplay data within 30 days.

Note: Deletion is permanent. Once your data is removed, it cannot be recovered, and your game progress will be lost.
